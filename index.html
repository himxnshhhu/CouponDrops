At Coupon Drops, we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when creating an account, uploading coupons, completing KYC verification, or contacting support. This includes:

  • Personal identification (name, email, phone number, date of birth)
  • Government-issued ID documents (for KYC verification only)
  • Bank account details (encrypted, for cashback withdrawals)
  • Transaction and activity data on the platform
  • Device and browser information for security purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to provide, improve, and personalise our services. Specifically, this includes processing transactions, verifying identity, preventing fraud, sending important notices, and improving platform features.

  • Processing coupon purchases and wallet transactions
  • Completing KYC verification and cashback withdrawals
  • Preventing fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Sending transactional and service-related communications
  • Personalising your deal recommendations

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share limited data with trusted service providers who assist in platform operations (payment processors, KYC verification services, cloud infrastructure), subject to strict confidentiality agreements.

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government authority, or to protect the safety of our users or the public.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time via Settings > Danger Zone, subject to legal retention requirements.

5. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data; object to or restrict processing; receive a copy of your data in a portable format; and withdraw consent at any time.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate and improve our platform, remember your preferences, and analyse usage patterns. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including AES-256 encryption for sensitive data, TLS for data in transit, two-factor authentication options, and regular security audits.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, requests, or complaints, please contact our Data Protection Officer at: privacy@coupondrops.in
